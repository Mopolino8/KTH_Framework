subroutine avgs_init ( )

Initialise spatial averaging of elements.

This routine performs initialistion of the satial averaging tool. It generated local and global mapping of elements depending on the original and averaged field dimensions.

Remarks
This routine uses global scratch space SCRNS, SCRVH, SCRUZ.
